Bio

Tyrelm is a singer-songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist from Nairobi, Kenya. His music blends Soul, R&B, and Blues with emotional lyrics, acoustic guitar, and warm, reflective vocals. He writes, records, mixes, and masters his own work using Ableton Live — every track is owned 100% end-to-end (master, publishing, and performance rights).

He runs Tyrelm Nation, a creative community hub for art, music, and poetry in Nairobi. His sound moves between intimate acoustic confessionals and full-bodied R&B productions, making each release feel personal and intentional.

Album Time And Space (2025) explores love, loss, and self-discovery across 12 tracks. The BROKEN DIARY EP (2026) strips things back — raw, vulnerable, and deeply human. Recent singles include Mwanaume, Dear Ty, and Kimbia (with JILLO).
